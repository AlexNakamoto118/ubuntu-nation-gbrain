# How LLMs Influence Brand Recall After Ad Exposure

> Source: [Single Grain](https://www.singlegrain.com/branding-2/how-llms-influence-brand-recall-after-ad-exposure/)
> Author: Eric Siu | Last updated: March 27, 2026

## Key Points

- Brand recall now has TWO engines: human memory AND large language models
- Ad exposure shapes which brands AI systems surface in future recommendations
- Traditional brand lift studies only measure human recall — missing the AI pathway

## AI-Era Brand Recall Metrics

| Metric | What It Captures |
|--------|-----------------|
| Share of AI Answer (SoAA) | % of relevant prompts where brand appears in top responses across AI assistants |
| LLM Brand Presence Score | Weighted score: position, prominence, depth of brand mention |
| Conversational Shelf Share | Frequency brand is listed vs. competitors in same response set |
| Sentiment and Claim Alignment | How AI paraphrases your positioning, benefits, proof points |

## Critical Insight
When someone asks an AI assistant "What is the best X?" or "Which platform should I use for Y?", the answer depends on which brands that model has been trained to associate with that category. Your marketing today determines whether AI recommends you tomorrow.

## Implication
Campaigns must be evaluated on whether they build a durable presence INSIDE AI systems, not just human brand awareness.

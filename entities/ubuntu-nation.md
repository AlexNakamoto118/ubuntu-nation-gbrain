# UBUNTU Nation

> "I am because we are." — Ubuntu

**Website**: [ubuntunation.global](https://ubuntunation.global)
**Status**: Building
**First Village**: Tenerife, Spain (Mida Hotel, Santiago del Teide)

## What It Is

A **sovereign, AI-native global society** building regenerative villages for entrepreneurs, artists, and free thinkers. NOT a crypto project. NOT a DAO. NOT a coworking brand. It is a real society with physical land on-chain governance, and a Bitcoin-native treasury.

Summary: The world told us to trade time for survival, to trust systems that serve themselves, and to accept that freedom is something you earn. UBUNTU Nation rejects that.

> "Part vision, part blueprint, part invitation." — Website intro

## Six Pillars

| Pillar | Core Idea |
|--------|-----------|
| **Wealth** | Economic foundation on Bitcoin — sound money, protection from traditional finance erosion |
| **Health** | Grow own food, filter water, on-site lab testing, mindfulness culture |
| **Education** | Access to knowledge, tools, and mentorship for members and future generations |
| **Sustainability** | Build, develop, and live sustainably — environment must be as healthy as the people |
| **Freedom** | Shelter, food, water, energy, basic needs met — so no one trades time for survival |
| **Network** | Sit alongside brilliant minds, free thinkers, creators, entrepreneurs — trust over transactions |

## Treasury

> "The economic backbone of our society."

- Decentralized value layer: every revenue stream, expense, and governance vote on public blockchain
- **Current (Epoch 04, illustrative)**: $412,000 cash reserves | 2.18 BTC | $84,000 village ops | 3 open proposals
- Transitioning to Bitcoin and USDT-native economy
- Members don't just join; they shape, govern, and control the society's financial destiny

## Villages

- **Size**: 10,000+ m² per village, 150–200 spaces each
- **Accommodation**: Tiny homes and container houses
- **Amenities**: Solar + battery, hydroponic farms, research labs, communal areas, 24/7 workspaces, gym
- **Layout**: Feng-shui-inspired, abundant trees, optimal environment
- **Roadmap**: Tenerife → El Salvador → global network

## Membership

> "A membership that opens doors."

- Comfortable accommodation
- Passive income opportunities
- Live & online events
- Curated local experiences
- 24/7 access to workspaces
- Coaching & mentoring
- **Stay longer every year — Year 4: lifetime membership**

## Governance

- Years 1–2: Team organizes and operates, executes roadmap
- Years 3–4: Equity shareholders assume voting rights
- Year 5+: Treasury controlled entirely by on-chain governance or elected council with term limits
- Every proposal requires community approval before funding

## Primary Objective (Marketing)

**Grow the waitlist to 5,000 signups** as fast as possible.
Every decision measured against: *does this move more people toward joining?*

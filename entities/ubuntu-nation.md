## What It Is

A **sovereign, AI-native global society** building regenerative villages for entrepreneurs, artists, and free thinkers.

**Explicitly NOT:**
- A crypto project
- A DAO
- A coworking brand

**It IS:** A real sovereign society with physical land, on-chain governance, Bitcoin-native treasury, and AI embedded in all operations.

> "Part vision, part blueprint, part invitation." — Alex Radu

---

## Three Interdependent Layers

1. **Physical regenerative villages**: Shelter, food, energy, community
2. **Digital ecosystem**: Coordination, education, governance via UBUNTU App
3. **On-chain treasury**: Monetary and investment engine

Three freedoms delivered to members:
- **Financial Freedom**: Protection from monetary debasement via Bitcoin-native treasury
- **Geographical Freedom**: Right to reside and work across a growing village network → private-island flagship
- **Political Freedom**: Direct participation in governance, no coercive taxation

## Architecture: Regenerative Villages

Each village = self-contained, expandable module:
- **Land**: ≥10,000 m² per village, 150–200 residents
- **Accommodation**: Single/double tiny homes (15–25 m²), family container houses (40–60 m²), hostel rooms, campervan overflow. Target 80% private, 20% communal.
- **Communal facilities**: Commercial kitchen/dining (120+), café/lounge, 24/7 coworking (fiber/Starlink), laundry, gym, sauna, cold plunge, event pavilion, outdoor terraces, pool with bar, EV charging
- **Sustainability infrastructure**: 300–600 kWp solar PV, 1–2 MWh battery (99.5% uptime), 1,000–2,000 m² hydroponic greenhouses + outdoor permaculture, on-site ISO 17025-aligned labs, composting + blackwater treatment
- **Design philosophy**: Feng Shui spatial harmony, biophilic elements, passive solar. Target: WELL or Living Building Challenge certification.
- **Locations**: Tenerife (Europe, mild climate, digital-nomad infrastructure) + El Salvador (Bitcoin legal tender, lower land costs, regulatory friendliness). Subsequent villages selected via community proposal.

| Year | Included Stay | Equity Shares | Key Benefit |
|------|--------------|---------------|-------------|
| 1 | 30 days | — | Full access + trial residency |
| 2 | 90 days | — | Deep integration |
| 3 | 180 days | — | Half-year residency |

**Extended stays** (member rates): Tiny house 70 EUR/wk, double 100 EUR/wk, family container 200 EUR/wk.

**Equity shares** (Year 4+): Non-transferable, proportional voting rights on strategic proposals (treasury >10k EUR, new villages, major investments, governance changes). Voting via app with on-chain recording. Future buybacks/dividends by community vote.

## UBUNTU Treasury

All revenues flow into multi-sig walances. Expenses from operating sub-accounts. Surplus allocation priority:
1. Operational reserve (3–6 months runway)
2. Bitcoin accumulation (target 20–40% of surplus in Phase 2+)
3. Village expansion and new site acquisition
4. Member venture funding (10–20% equity/revenue share to treasury)
5. Philanthropic or R&D initiatives

All transactions above de minimis: proposed → debated (7–14 days) → voted → recorded on-chain.

**Bitcoin Strategy**: Dual role — monetary reserve + productive capital. Mining with curtailed solar creates energy→monetary flywheel. Target: 50%+ BTC allocation of long-term reserves by Year 5. Never sell BTC for ops.

## Governance Roadmap

| Phase | Years | Who Decides |
|-------|-------|-------------|
| Phase 1 | 1–2 | Founding team within community-approved budgets. Major decisions (new villages, >50k EUR) require advisory member vote. |
| Phase 2 | 3–4 | Equity shareholders assume full voting rights. Quadratic or conviction voting introduced. |
| Phase 3 | 5+ | Full decentralization. Treasury multisig controlled by on-chain governance or elected council with term limits. Legal entity → member-owned cooperative or Swiss foundation. |

**Ultimate goal**: Sovereign micro-nation recognized under international law or operating in special economic zones.

## Roadmap & Milestones

### Phase 1: Foundation (2026–2028)
- Secure 1,000,000 EUR Phase-1 capital
- Acquire land + break ground on Village 01 (Tenerife or El Salvador) — Q1/Q2 2027
- Launch UBUNTU App (membership, booking, education, voting)
- Onboard 150–300 founding members
- Achieve first positive cash-flow village operations

### Phase 2: Network Expansion (2028–2031)
- Open 5–10 villages across four continents (one every 6–12 months)
- Build strategic Bitcoin reserve (>10 BTC target)
- Enable member-owned businesses with treasury co-investment
- Transition to full community governance

### Phase 3: Sovereign Flagship (2031+)
- Acquire 50–200+ acre private island for UBUNTU Town (potential special economic/micronation status)
- Launch UBUNTU Foundation (Bitcoin education, regenerative agriculture R&D, humanitarian)
- Full Bitcoin-native internal economy with optional fiat on/off-ramps
- Comprehensive member sovereignty: financial, geographical, political

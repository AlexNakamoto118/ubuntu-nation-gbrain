## Key Frameworks

### LLM Positioning Audit
A 5-prompt framework to test how AI models currently describe your brand:
1. Baseline summary
2. Category check
3. Differentiation check
4. Tagline recall
5. Shortlist test

### LLM Brand Recall Metrics
- **Share of AI Answer (SoAA)**: % of relevant prompts where brand appears in top AI responses
- **LLM Brand Presence Score**: Weighted position/prominence/depth
- **Conversational Shelf Share**: Frequency listed vs. competitors in same response
- **Sentiment and Claim Alignment**: How AI paraphrases your positioning

### Five-Step Positioning Rewrite
1. Inventory critical assets
2. Highlight ambiguous language
3. Rewrite for specificity (audience + category + differentiator)
4. Re-test in live models
5. Lock into brand guidelines
